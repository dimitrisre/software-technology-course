Programm:translate
version:1.0

This programm translates a text from an iso-8859-7 format to a iso-8859-1 format.
Functions like a pipe in the unix systems.Reads from standar input an writes to standar output.

eg

$: cat test.7 | translate

or

$: translate < test.7 > test.1
